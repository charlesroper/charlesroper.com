I have previously worked with wildlife conservation charities, environmental data centres, major theme parks, household tourist destinations, a major festival, a high street bank, a local college, a local community centre, a cathedral, and a spice island hotel in Zanzibar (among others).

I have worked as a technical lead, a developer, a graphic designer, a web designer, a biodiversity data manager, a GIS wrangler, a teacher, an IT technician, and a CIC director.
